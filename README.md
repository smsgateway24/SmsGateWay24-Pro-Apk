# SmsGateWay24 Android App

SmsGateWay24 is an Android application that turns your device into a messaging gateway.  
It allows you to send messages, track delivery status, and integrate your phone with your messaging infrastructure.

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

- Use your Android device as a messaging gateway
- Send messages via available channels
- Track delivery statuses
- Connect device to your account in minutes
- Configure webhooks and manage settings remotely
